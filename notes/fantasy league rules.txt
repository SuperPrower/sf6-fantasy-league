
>> SF6 Fantasy League Rules <<

	Player Market:
		- X players are available in the player market.
		- Players are seperated into different price tiers.
		- Players may shift tiers throughout the season in extenuating 
			circumstances.
		- Players may be added to the player market mid-season.
		- Should a team go over budget due to a price shift, their roster 
			remains legal.
		- A team must be legal (i.e. under budget) after any roster changes are
			made.

	Team Selection:
		- Players must be picked within the starting budget of X.
		- Players are not mutually exclusive, i.e. they can be shared across 
			teams.
		- A team must consist of exactly 5 players
	
	Events:
		- At the start of the season, three different tiers of events are
			determined.
		- Each tier of event has a different point scoring distribution.
		- The events of the season are known and planned ahead of time.
	
	Points:
		- After any predetermined scoring event concludes, team scores are
			updated.
		- Only the top 4 scoring players in every team are considered.
		- Lack of attendance is equal to no points.
		- The team with the most points at the end of a season is the winner.
		
	
	Roster Changes:
		- 4 trade windows occur within the year.
		- Rosters are locked outside of these trade windows.
		- During a trade window, each team can make up to 2 roster switches.
		- Team's must stay within budget throughout the entire season.
		- A team may choose to make no roster changes, or the maximum of 2
			changes each trade window.
		- If a player is retired mid-season, the team must wait until the next
			trade window to change their roster.
